PRIVACY POLICY
YOUR DATA IS YOURS
LAST UPDATED: MARCH 2026
OUR COMMITMENT
Embodying Sane was built to help people see clearly. We apply that same clarity to your data. We will never sell, rent, or exploit your personal information.
WHAT WE COLLECT
We collect your name and email address when you create an account. We collect payment information through Stripe (our payment processor) — we never see or store your full card number. We collect usage data such as how many analyses you have run, solely to manage your free trial and subscription.
WHAT WE DO NOT COLLECT
We do not store the conversations, messages, or personal reflections you enter into the app. This content is processed in real time to generate your analysis and is not retained on our servers after your session ends.
HOW WE USE YOUR DATA
Your email is used to manage your account, send subscription receipts, and provide account support. We may occasionally send product updates — you may opt out at any time.
AI PROCESSING
The AI features in this app are powered by Anthropic's Claude API. Text you submit for analysis is sent to Anthropic's servers for processing. Anthropic's privacy policy governs that processing. We do not store your submissions after the API returns your results.
YOUR RIGHTS
You have the right to access, correct, or delete your account data at any time. To request deletion of your account and all associated data, contact us directly. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.
COOKIES
This app does not use tracking cookies. We do not track you across websites.

For privacy questions or data requests, reach us through the contact option in your account settings.
